Who we are

Job hunt is a job search website which connects job seekers with employers. We gather many job opportunities of different categories from all over the world and put them in one place so that they are easily accessible. Job hunt is a place to search for jobs, research companies and get career advice. We strive to increase the working community all over the world.

Founder

Robel Tesfaye is a Software Engineer who graduated from AAiT with BS in Software Engineering. He founded job hunt inspired to solve the problem of luck of resources to search for jobs. He seeks to minimize unemployment issues through using his skills.
